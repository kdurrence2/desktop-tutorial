About REAL Art Gallery

REAL Art Gallery opened it's door to the Marietta, GA community in 2023. Led by Creative Director and Gallery Manager Art McArthur, our mission is to highlight artists within the community and fostering a love of art for the next generation. From established artists to budding students, from canvas paintings to metal sculptures, we welcome all types of art to our gallery.


Explore

With new exhibits and collections available each month, we hope that you will join us and explore fantastic, innovative artists. REAL Art Gallery is proud to offer artwork from artists of different races, cultures, backgrounds, and abilities. Experiencing new voices and perspectives through the medium of art is one of our greatest passions.


Engage

Community is the soul of our gallery. Each exhibit, collection, and artist is carefully selected to best capture and engage our community. We aim to bring artists, students, and people togehter to enjoy the wonders of the human experience. By bringing people togehter through art, we are able to enjoy more of what makes us people and neighbors.
